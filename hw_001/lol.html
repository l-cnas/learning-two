League of Legends

Riot Games

League of Legends is a competitive 5v5 strategy game where players,
called “champions,” battle to destroy the enemy team’s Nexus.
Each champion has unique abilities, and matches combine team coordination,
quick decision-making, and mechanical skill. The game also features an evolving
lore and professional esports scene worldwide.
