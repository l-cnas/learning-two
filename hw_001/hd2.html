Helldivers 2

Arrowhead Game Studios

Helldivers 2 is a chaotic, team-based sci-fi shooter where players
act as elite soldiers fighting to defend “Super Earth” from alien
threats across hostile planets. It emphasizes cooperation, friendly
fire realism, and coordinated strategy — every mission contributes to a
shared galactic war effort led by the player community.

Helldivers 2 main stuff

  • Terminids - insect-like aliens that swarm planets in huge numbers.
  • Automatons - aggressive robotic enemies with heavy armor and long-range weapons.
  • Illuminate - highly advanced alien civilization.
  • Super Earth – a militarized democracy promoting “Managed Freedom” across the galaxy.
  • Democracy and Liberty – the official cause of every Helldiver mission.
