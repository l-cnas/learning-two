EVE Online

CCP Games

EVE Online is a massive space-based MMORPG set in a persistent, player-driven universe. Players pilot customizable ships to mine, trade, explore, manufacture, fight pirates, or engage in large-scale PvP wars and politics. The entire in-game economy, corporations (guilds), and territorial control are run by players, leading to real-world-scale battles, espionage, and alliances. In short — it’s about freedom, economy, and power struggles in a single shared galaxy.
